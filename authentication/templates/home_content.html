About SafeFace

SafeFace is is designed to let you create and use avatars without the risk of harmful deep fakes. Generate and save unique characters and swap their faces with any image or video. We allow users to protect their identity online, while becoming anyone they want. Our generation ensures that no real faces can be used in face swaps, preventing unethical use.

Why SafeFace is different:

  • Ethical and legal: No real face swaps, only generated characters
  • Privacy-focused: Your data is safe and secure
  • User-friendly: Easy to create, save, and use avatars

Join us in making face swaps fun, safe, and ethical!
