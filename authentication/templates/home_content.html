About SafeFace

SafeFace is a moral and legal face swap app designed to let you create and use avatars without the risk of harmful deep fakes. By selecting a range of attributes, you can generate unique characters and swap their faces with any target image. Our focus on ethical usage ensures that no real faces can be used in face swaps, preventing potential misuse.

Our current features include:

  • Create avatars by selecting specific attributes
  • Save and manage your custom avatars
  • Swap faces with target images

Future features we are working on include:

  • Face swapping in videos
  • Enhanced avatar customization options
  • Improved user interface and experience

Why SafeFace is different:

  • Ethical and legal: No real face swaps, only generated characters
  • Privacy-focused: Your data is safe and secure
  • User-friendly: Easy to create, save, and use avatars

Join us in making face swaps fun, safe, and ethical!
